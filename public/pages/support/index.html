How do I convert files on Pushtoconverter?

Converting files is simple! Just select the tool you need from our homepage, upload your file, and click convert. The process is automatic and your converted file will be ready for download within seconds.

Is Pushtoconverter really free?

Yes! Pushtoconverter is completely free to use. We don't charge any fees, require registration, or have hidden costs. All our conversion tools are available at no cost, forever.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. All files are processed securely with SSL encryption and are automatically deleted from our servers after conversion. We never store or share your personal files.

What file formats do you support?

We support over 40 different file formats including video (MP4, AVI, MOV), audio (MP3, WAV, FLAC), images (JPG, PNG, WEBP), and documents (PDF, DOCX, XLSX). Check our tools page for the complete list.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, we have a maximum file size limit of 100MB per file. This ensures fast processing and optimal performance for all users. For larger files, we recommend compressing them first.

How long do converted files stay available?

Converted files are available for download for 24 hours after conversion. After this period, they are automatically deleted from our servers to maintain security and free up storage space.

Can I use Pushtoconverter on mobile devices?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can convert files from any device with an internet connection.

What if my conversion fails?

If a conversion fails, please check that your file is in a supported format and under 100MB. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or contact our support team for assistance.
